# Privacy Policy — Kioskeo

**Last updated:** April 12, 2026

## Introduction

Kioskeo ("the App") is developed by EgeaINC. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** The App operates entirely on your device.

### What the App does NOT do:
- Does not collect personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not display advertisements
- Does not share any data with third parties
- Does not use cookies or similar technologies

### Local Storage Only

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- **Products:** Product names, barcodes, prices, stock levels, and images (stored in local SQLite database)
- **Sales:** Sale records, payment methods, and amounts (stored in local SQLite database)
- **Clients:** Client names, phone numbers, and account balances for store credit (stored in local SQLite database)
- **Categories:** Product category assignments (stored in local SQLite database)
- **Preferences:** App settings like theme and business name (stored in SharedPreferences)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

### Camera Usage

The App uses the device camera for barcode scanning to identify products. **Images are processed on-device only using Google ML Kit and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.**

### Internet Usage

The App connects to the internet solely for:

1. **In-app purchases:** Processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We do not have access to your payment information. Google's privacy policy applies to these transactions.

### Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:
- **Camera:** Used for scanning product barcodes. Images are processed on-device only and are never recorded or transmitted.
- **Internet:** Required for in-app purchases through Google Play.
- **Storage:** Used for importing product lists (PDF/Excel/CSV) and exporting/importing database backups. Files are processed locally only.

## Backup & Data Export

The App allows you to export your database as a backup file. This file is shared using Android's native share functionality — you choose where to save it (Google Drive, OneDrive, etc.). **EgeaINC does not have access to your backup files.**

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. All data is stored locally on the user's device only and is never transmitted.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
